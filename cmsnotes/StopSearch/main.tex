  \begin{abstract}

%keep it free of newcommands for eventual upload

This note describes a refinement of the search for new physics in the 
dilepton final state of Z plus two or more jets plus missing transverse 
energy (MET) (SUS-11-021).
The update uses the same data, same selection criteria, and same analysis methodology,
but introduces additional requirements that significantly reduce the backgrounds
at the cost of making the analysis less general.
The most important such requirement is that we veto events containing b-tagged jets. 
In addition, a dijet invariant mass consistent with a W or Z is required, and we veto events with a third lepton.
This new search is thus focused on the WZ plus MET and ZZ plus MET final states, and is motived by the fact that these final states
are common in chargino neutralino and neutralino pair production.
We see no excess above the background prediction, and place model independent
upper limits on the production of these final states as a function of the neutralino mass.

\end{abstract}
